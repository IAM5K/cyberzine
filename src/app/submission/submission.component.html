Submission Rules

Before submitting any content please read the rules carefully.
  1. Writers must fill the attached form before submitting any content for the magazine.
  2. We request the writers to submit original and unique content only.
  3. Before the submission of the content, authors must make sure that their content is free of any factual inaccuracies.
  4. Any form of interactive as well as informative content (articles, puzzles, news clippings, reports, research work, projects, etc.) shall be accepted as adequate submissions.
  5. You will receive a Google Drive Folder link via email after the submission of the above-mentioned form. All the content that you wish to submit must be uploaded to this folder.
  6. The main content must only be submitted as a text file or Google document. Any other type of file submission will automatically be rejected.
  7. All supporting images must be of the highest quality available and should be added to the folder separately.
    • Do not submit stock images with watermarks.
    • Do not submit images that are not related to the content.
    • Do not submit any images you do not have the license to use.
  8. All the external sources that the content is taken from must be mentioned under sources.
  9. Classic puzzles cannot have more than one writer. Additional writers are allowed only if the content is of a non-classic puzzle type.
  10. All the aforementioned rules are strictly enforced and non-conformance will lead to immediate rejection of the content in question.
  11. In case of conflict, the decision of the associated faculty and the managing team will be final.
  12. The deadline for submission is one month from submitting the google form.
  13. If the content has been published previously on any website and you are the owner of the above–mentioned content, please include the following disclaimer and a link to the original post in the sources document. We will contact you later with a copyright transfer form for the same.
